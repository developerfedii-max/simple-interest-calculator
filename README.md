# Simple Interest Calculator

## 📌 Description
This project explains a **Simple Interest Calculator**, which is used to calculate the interest earned or paid on a principal amount over a specific period of time at a fixed interest rate.

---

## 🧮 Formula Used
The formula for Simple Interest is:

Simple Interest (SI) = (P × R × T) / 100

Where:
- **P** = Principal Amount  
- **R** = Rate of Interest (per year)  
- **T** = Time (in years)

---

## 🧾 Example Calculation
If:
- Principal = 10,000  
- Rate = 5%  
- Time = 2 years  

Then:

SI = (10000 × 5 × 2) / 100  
SI = 1000

---

## 💡 Use Case
Simple Interest is commonly used in:
- Basic banking calculations  
- Short-term loans  
- Educational math examples  

---

## 🚀 How It Works
1. Enter the principal amount  
2. Enter the interest rate  
3. Enter the time period  
4. Apply the formula to get the simple interest
